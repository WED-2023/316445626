about me


Welcome to my website!

I'm Yanni, and I'm thrilled that you're here. I was born in Kalamata, Greece, and music has been my passion since I was a young boy. My music is a reflection of my life experiences, my travels, and my connection to the world around me. I believe that music has the power to heal and inspire, and it brings me great joy to share my compositions with others.

Throughout my career, I have been fortunate to work with some incredibly talented musicians and travel to many beautiful places. I hope that my music will touch your heart and enrich your life. Thank you for visiting my website!
